Loved One(s) Passport(s)

The "Loved One Passport" is where you document who your loved one is and what her/his condition is. It is accessible only to you but you will want to share it with the caregivers of your choice so that they care for you loved one(s) the way you want them to.

It's important to update this passport when the Loved One's situation changes (example, they had surgery, lost their dentures, new hearing aid, etc.)
